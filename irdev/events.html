Cultural Events

Experience the rich tapestry of Indian culture through vibrant festivals and celebrations that bring our diverse community together at IIT Jammu

Diwali

Festival of Lights

The most awaited festival at IIT Jammu, celebrated with rangoli competitions, traditional dances, and a spectacular fireworks display that illuminates the entire campus.

Holi

Festival of Colors

A joyous celebration where the campus comes alive with vibrant colors, traditional sweets, folk music, and students from all backgrounds unite in the spirit of togetherness.

Ganesh Chaturthi

Lord of New Beginnings

A spiritually enriching celebration featuring beautiful Ganesh idols, devotional songs, traditional prasadam distribution, and cultural performances honoring Lord Ganesha.

Raksha Bandhan

Bond of Protection

A heartwarming celebration of sibling bonds where students tie rakhis, share sweets, and create a family atmosphere on campus for those away from home.

Independence Day

National Pride

A patriotic celebration featuring flag hoisting, cultural performances, speeches honoring freedom fighters, and exhibitions showcasing India's journey to independence.

Republic Day

Constitutional Celebration

A grand commemoration of our Constitution with parade ceremonies, cultural programs highlighting diverse Indian traditions, and discussions on democratic values.
