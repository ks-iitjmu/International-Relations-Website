Sports & Recreation

Discover the vibrant sports culture at IIT Jammu through our Sports fests and clubs

Sports Fests

Annual sporting events that bring together athletes from across the campus and beyond

Pahal

Fresher's Sports Fest

Pahal helps freshers break the ice and connect with their seniors and peers.

Pravaah

Inter-Department Sports Fest

Pravaah is a branch-vs-branch event where students compete with spirit.

Inter-Gen

InterYear Sports Fest

InterGen is a Inter Year contest where all students from different batches compete in teams.

Convoquer

Inter-College Sports Fest

Convoquer is an inter-college contest with teams from Jammu colleges.

Sports Clubs

Active sports clubs fostering athletic excellence and team spirit

Cricket Club

Premier Cricket Team

Represents IIT Jammu in inter-collegiate tournaments and conducts regular practice sessions.

Football Club

Soccer Excellence

Dedicated to promoting football culture with regular training and competitive matches.

Basketball Club

Court Champions

Focuses on developing basketball skills and participating in regional championships.

Badminton Club

Smash & Rally

Offers a mix of recreational and competitive play, with sessions for skill development and fitness.

Pool Club

Cue Control

Provides a relaxing yet competitive environment for cue sports enthusiasts to improve and compete. training.

Table Tennis Club

Ping Pong Pros

Provides platform for table tennis enthusiasts to improve skills and compete.

Athletics Club

Track & Field

Focuses on track and field events, training for various athletic competitions.

Volleyball Club

Net Warriors

Promotes volleyball through regular practice sessions and inter-college competitions.

Chess Club

Strategic Masters

Encourages strategic thinking and organizes regular chess tournaments and workshops.

Indoor Games Club

Fun & Focus

Hosts a variety of indoor games like carrom, darts, and board games for recreation and competition.

Esports Club

Digital Arena

Brings together gaming enthusiasts for tournaments, team play, and skill development in popular esports titles.

Sports Facilities

State-of-the-art facilities to support athletic excellence and recreational activities

Gymnasium

Well-equipped gym with modern fitness machines, free weights, and training space.

Basketball Court

Outdoor and indoor courts for regular practice and tournaments.

Volleyball Court

Dedicated sand and hard-surface courts for competitive and recreational play.

Football Field

Well maintained football field with proper markings and goal posts.

Cricket Practice Nets

Practice nets with turf pitch for regular training and skill improvement.

Multi-Purpose Sports Complex

Indoor facility for badminton, table tennis, pool, squash and recreational activities.
